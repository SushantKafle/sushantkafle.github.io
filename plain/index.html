I am a first year Ph.D. student in Computing and Information Science at the Golisano College of Computing and Information Science, Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). I am also working as a research assistant at LATLab RIT supervised by Professor Matt Huenerfauth. My current work is a part of a research project on generating real-time captions during live meetings for people who are deaf or hard of hearing (DHH), where I am investigating ways to optimize Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) system for the task of captioning for DHH users.

Research Interests: Human-Computer Interaction, Accessiblity, Speech Processing, Natural Language Processing.
