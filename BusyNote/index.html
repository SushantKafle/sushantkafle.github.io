About

Ever had moments when you wished to notify your callers that you are busy and you are not able to answer them? When you are driving on your vehicle, do you want to let the callers know you are driving? Well, here's the app that does just what you wanted.

BusyNote is a free application created by Sushant Kafle. You can find it in Google Play Store for android devices. Please let me know how you feel about the application and about any features you would like to see.
